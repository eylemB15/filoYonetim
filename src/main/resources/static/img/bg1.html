FLEET MANAGEMENT




According to the request of our existing clients, our parent company, DBS 5 years ago established the sector for GPS / GLONASS vehicle tracking and telematics. In cooperation with partners and taking into account the needs of the market, we decided to offer approved and quality solutions from all over the world.
Since then until today, by using high-quality devices, flexible and on the client demand adaptable software and excellent support, we have gained the trust of more than 150 companies in Serbia and Bosnia and Herzegovina. By everyday learning and improving, we use our knowledge into the most modern systems which our customers bring not only big savings but also they solve system problems. Our Monitoring Center which works 24/7 provides support to customers at every moment and brings additional safety at exploitation of the system.

Trend of automation and optimization grows of working processes, DBS has been specialized in the following segments:
- GPS / GLONASS vehicle tracking
- Managing and optimization of vehicle fleet
- More efficient exploitation of machinery
- Telemetry of working processes and stationary machines
- Safety of workers in the field
- Monitoring of goods
- Mobile video surveillance
